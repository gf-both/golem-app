## The Three Hard Blockers Around Bare "GOLEM"

### 1. USPTO Trademark Conflicts (HIGH RISK)
Live registered marks containing "GOLEM" in software-relevant classes:

| Mark | Owner | Class | Status |
|------|-------|-------|--------|
| GOLEM | Highwire Games LLC | Class 9 (VR game software) | Live, Reg #5955761 |
| GOLEM | Golem Factory GmbH (blockchain) | Class 9/42 | Live, Serial #79222597 |
| GOLEM CREATIONS | Golem Creations LLC | Class 41 (entertainment) | Live, Serial #88583446 |

A filing for bare "GOLEM" in Class 9 or 42 is very likely to draw a **Section 2(d) likelihood-of-confusion refusal**. Modifiers like "GOLEM AI" don't help much because "AI" is descriptive and disclaimed — the dominant element stays "GOLEM." Stronger options: coined compounds (GOLEMOS, GOLEMIC) or non-descriptive prefix verbs (TRY GOLEM, GET GOLEM).

### 2. Existing Brand Collisions (HIGH RISK)
Three direct collisions in your category space:

- **Golem App** (Avocado Technologies, iOS) — an AI productivity app that turns tasks into RPG quests with XP and stat tracking. **Nearly identical product positioning to GOLEM.** A user Googling "golem app" today lands on them.
- **Golem Cloud** (Ziverge) — agentic AI infrastructure. Directly owns the "Golem + AI" mindshare.
- **Golem Network** (GLM token, $0.14) — pivoted to agentic AI in 2026, partnered with Salad.com. Crypto-adjacent press will create confusion.

Plus **golem.de** (top-tier German tech news site) and **golem.ai** (French NLP) lock the SERP outside the US.

### 3. SEO Saturation (UNWINNABLE)
"Golem," "golem app," "golem ai" are all owned by Wikipedia (mythology), golem.de, Golem Network's AI page, or the iOS Golem App. Realistic ranking for any in <18 months is not happening. You'll need to win on category keywords (`cosmic signature`, `self-knowledge platform`, `framework synthesis`, `relationship intelligence`) — not on the brand keyword.

**Single-member LLC tax note:** by default, a single-member LLC is a "disregarded entity" for federal tax — profits flow to your personal 1040 Schedule C. You can later elect S-Corp treatment (Form 2553) if income justifies it (usually past ~$50K/yr net), which can save self-employment tax. Don't worry about this until after first revenue.

**Stripe Atlas notes for LLC formation:**
- Atlas DOES support Delaware LLC formation (used to be C-Corp only — LLC option added 2023)
- You'll get: certificate of formation, EIN, operating agreement template, registered agent for year 1 included
- Year 2+ you pay ~$50/year Delaware franchise tax + ~$125/year registered agent (Atlas-included or switch to Northwest/Harbor)
- Atlas Stripe account is provisioned automatically — connects to your live Stripe payments

## Trademark Filing Recommendation

File **two marks** for layered protection:

1. **TRY GOLEM** (standard character word mark) in Class 9 + Class 42 — ~$350/class via USPTO TEAS Plus, ~$700 total. Higher clearance odds because "TRY GOLEM" as a phrase is distinguishable from the bare "GOLEM" marks already registered.
2. **GOLEM** stylized logo (your visual mark) in Class 9 + Class 42 — same fee structure. The stylization is what gets you protection even when the word mark itself is contested.

**Do not file bare "GOLEM" as a standard character word mark.** It will draw a 2(d) refusal and you lose the filing fee.

If budget allows, spend $400–800 on a formal attorney clearance opinion before filing. Stripe Atlas has TM filing add-ons but they're often a thin wrapper — for a contested term like GOLEM, an actual TM attorney's eyes are worth the money.
